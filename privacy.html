Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

At SplitEven, we believe your personal information belongs to you. We’ve designed our app to be simple and transparent, collecting only the information needed to help you split bills effortlessly with your friends.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Friend Names: When you add friends to a split, we save their names so you can find them easily next time.
  • Receipt Photos: When you scan a receipt, the image is sent to our secure servers to allow our AI to read the items and prices for you.
  • Contact Info: If you send us a message through our Support Center, we keep a record of your email to help solve your issue.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Unique ID: We use a secure, anonymous ID (via Firebase) to keep your receipt history organized just for you.
  • Usage Data: We look at basic, anonymous info (like which buttons are clicked most) to help make the app better.
  • Crash Reports: If the app stops working, we get a technical report so we can fix the bug.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to make bill splitting fast and fair:

  • To turn your receipt photos into an itemized list using our AI.
  • To show you your past split history when you use the app.
  • To calculate the exact tax and tip for each person in your group.

Our Privacy Promise

We will never:

  • Sell your personal information to anyone.
  • Share your private receipts with other people.
  • Read your private messages when you share a split summary.
  • Track your physical location.

Advertising

To keep the core features of SplitEven free for everyone, we show ads using Google AdMob. You may see two types of ads:

  • Interstitial Ads: Full-screen ads that appear occasionally after a split is completed to avoid interrupting your workflow.
  • Rewarded Ads: Optional ads you can choose to watch to earn extra receipt scans.

These ad services may use an anonymous ID from your device to show you relevant ads, but SplitEven does not share your specific receipt data or friend lists with advertisers.

Data Storage & Security

We take your data security seriously:

  • Cloud Sync: Your history is tied to a secure ID on your phone. However, if you uninstall the app, this history will be deleted and cannot be recovered.
  • Server Processing: While receipt images are sent to our server to be read by AI, they are not shared with third parties for marketing.

Your Rights

You are in control of your data. You can:

  • You can clear your local app data at any time through your phone settings to reset your anonymous ID.
  • Contact us if you have questions about how your data is handled.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we'll notify you through the app. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
