Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At SplitEven, we believe that your personal information belongs to you. We've built our app with privacy as a core principle, collecting only what's necessary to provide you with a great bill-splitting experience.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Friend Names: When you add friends to split bills with, we store their names locally on your device so you can easily select them for future splits.
  • Receipt Data: When you scan a receipt, the item names, prices, and totals are extracted and stored temporarily on your device to calculate the split.
  • Support Communications: If you contact us for support, we keep records of our conversations to help resolve your issues.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: We collect basic information about how you use the app (like which features you use most) to help us improve SplitEven. This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you.
  • Device Information: We may collect general device information (like device type and operating system version) to ensure the app works properly on your device.
  • Crash Reports: If the app crashes, we receive anonymous error reports to help us fix bugs and improve stability.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the bill-splitting features you use
  • Remember your friends for easier future splits
  • Improve the app based on how people use it
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Respond to your support requests

What We Don't Do

We want to be clear about what we will never do with your information:

  • We don't sell your personal information to anyone
  • We don't store your receipt images on our servers
  • We don't share your data with advertisers
  • We don't track your location
  • We don't read the contents of your messages when you share splits

Data Storage

Most of your data (including your friends list and split history) is stored locally on your device, not on our servers. This means:

  • Your data stays with you and under your control
  • If you delete the app, your local data is deleted too
  • You can clear your data anytime from the app settings

Third-Party Services

SplitEven may use trusted third-party services to help provide certain features. These services have their own privacy policies and handle data according to their terms. We only work with services that meet our privacy standards.

Children's Privacy

SplitEven is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics collection (in app settings)
  • Contact us with privacy questions or concerns

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we'll notify you through the app or by other means. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@spliteven.app

We take your privacy seriously and will respond to your inquiries promptly.
