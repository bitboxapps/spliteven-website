Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

SplitEven makes splitting bills easy in just a few steps: Take a photo of your receipt, and our AI automatically reads all the items and prices. Then add the friends who are splitting the bill, tap to assign items to each person, and SplitEven calculates exactly what everyone owes including their fair share of tax and tip.
Yes! SplitEven is free to download and use. You can scan receipts and split bills without any subscription or payment required. We may offer optional premium features in the future, but the core bill-splitting functionality will always be free.
SplitEven works with most printed receipts from restaurants, cafes, bars, and retail stores. For best results, make sure the receipt is flat, well-lit, and the text is clearly visible. The app supports receipts in multiple languages and currencies.
Yes, you can easily edit any item name, price, or quantity after scanning. You can also add items manually if something was missed. We want to make sure your split is always accurate.
Tax and tip are split proportionally based on the total of items each person is paying for. For example, if you ordered $30 worth of food and your friend ordered $20, you would pay 60% of the tax and tip while your friend pays 40%. This ensures everyone pays their fair share.
Absolutely! If multiple people shared an item (like a pizza or appetizer), you can assign that item to all the people who shared it. The cost will be divided equally among them automatically.
After calculating the split, you can share each person's total via text message, email, WhatsApp, or any other messaging app on your phone. The shared message includes a clear breakdown of what they owe.
No. Only one person needs SplitEven to scan and split the bill. You can easily share each person’s amount through messaging apps so everyone knows what to pay.
Yes! If your receipt doesn't include a tip or you want to adjust it, you can easily add or modify the tip amount. The new tip will be split proportionally among everyone just like the tax.
Receipts are stored only on your current app installation. If you uninstall the app or switch to a new device, your past receipts may not be recoverable. In future updates, you’ll be able to sign in and access your receipts across devices.
