Best Bill Splitting Apps for iPhone in 2026

📅 24 May 2026 ⏱️ 7 min read ✍️ SplitEven Team
Quick Answer

The best bill splitting apps for iPhone in 2026 are SplitEven (AI receipt scanner, free, friends view without installing the app), Splitwise (long-term expense tracking with the same group), and Tricount (group travel). For splitting a single restaurant bill as fast as possible, SplitEven is the top pick.

Splitting a bill on iPhone should take 30 seconds, not 10 minutes of mental arithmetic and awkward side conversations. The best bill splitting apps for iOS handle the maths automatically — whether you need to scan a restaurant receipt, divide a group grocery run, or track who's owed what across a whole trip.

We tested the most popular expense sharing apps available on iOS in 2026. Here's what each one actually does well, and when to reach for it.

What Makes a Good iPhone Bill Splitter?

  • Speed at the table — the best iOS bill splitting apps let you go from receipt to settled in under a minute
  • Receipt scanning — manually typing each item is slow and error-prone; AI scanning removes the friction entirely
  • Proportional tax and tip — splitting equally is fine for identical orders; proportional is fairer when people ordered different things
  • Multi-currency support — essential if you travel internationally with friends
  • No forced sign-up for friends — the best apps don't require everyone to create an account just to see what they owe

The 5 Best Bill Splitting Apps for iPhone

⚡

SplitEven

Best for: Restaurant bills, receipt scanning, and instant sharing

SplitEven is built for the moment the bill arrives. Point your iPhone camera at any receipt and the AI reads every item, price, tax, and tip automatically — even detecting the currency. You assign items to people, and it calculates each person's exact share in seconds.

What sets it apart from other iOS expense apps is the sharing experience. You can send a link so friends view the full itemised breakdown in their browser, export it as an image, or share a text summary — all via WhatsApp, iMessage, or any other app. No account required for your friends.

✓ Strengths
AI receipt scanner, proportional tax & tip, multi-currency detection, share as link or image, full receipt history, no sign-up for friends
✗ Limitations
Focused on in-the-moment splitting rather than long-term group debt tracking
💸

Splitwise

Best for: Tracking ongoing balances between friends

Splitwise is the most established expense tracking app on iOS, and it's genuinely excellent for ongoing shared expenses between the same group of people — rent, shared subscriptions, weekly dinners. It remembers every balance and simplifies who owes what over time.

The free version now has daily expense limits and shows ads between screens. The core experience is better on Splitwise Pro, which costs $4.99/month. It doesn't have receipt scanning, so every expense is entered manually.

✓ Strengths
Long-term balance tracking, debt simplification, large user base, reliable
✗ Limitations
No receipt scanning, manual entry only, free tier has daily expense limits and ads
✈️

Tricount

Best for: Group holidays and travel

Tricount is purpose-built for group travel. Each person logs what they paid throughout the trip, and Tricount works out the optimal settlement at the end — minimising the number of transfers needed. No account required. Multi-currency support is included for free, making it a solid iPhone expense app for international groups.

✓ Strengths
No account required, settlement optimisation, good for trips, multi-currency free
✗ Limitations
No receipt scanning, not ideal for splitting a single bill at a restaurant
📊

Splid

Best for: Clean design and offline use

Splid is a polished iOS expense splitting app with support for over 150 currencies. It works offline, requires no account to start, and has a particularly clean interface. It's a strong Splitwise alternative for groups who want something lighter. Like most trackers, it doesn't scan receipts — you enter expenses manually.

✓ Strengths
150+ currencies, offline support, no account needed, clean UI
✗ Limitations
No receipt scanning, less widely known so friends may need encouragement to join
🧾

Kittysplit

Best for: Zero friction, one-off group expenses

Kittysplit is completely free, has no app to download, and requires no account — not even for the people joining your group. You share a link, everyone logs their expenses, and it tells everyone what to pay. It's the lowest-friction option for a one-off trip or event where you don't want anyone to have to install anything.

✓ Strengths
Completely free, no account for anyone, no app to download, web-based
✗ Limitations
No receipt scanning, no mobile app, limited to simple expense logging

Which iPhone Bill Splitting App Should You Use?

  • Splitting a restaurant or takeaway bill right now → SplitEven. Scan the receipt, assign items, share the breakdown in under a minute.
  • Tracking shared expenses with the same friends over months → Splitwise (if you don't mind the free tier limits) or Splid (if you want something cleaner and fully free).
  • A group holiday where everyone pays different things → Tricount. Built exactly for this use case.
  • A one-off group event with no friction for anyone → Kittysplit. No app, no account, just a shared link.

Many people end up using two: SplitEven for in-the-moment bill splitting at restaurants, and Tricount or Splid for tracking longer group trips. They solve different problems and work well together.
